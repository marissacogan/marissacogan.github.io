PWHL Top 5 2024-25

Predictions based on the inaugural 2023-24 season.

The PWHL has 6 teams, and has just started their second season.

Let's look at the leaderboard from last season.

Ranked #1 is Natalie Spooner is a forward for the Toronto Sceptres. She was first ranked following the 2023-24 season with 20 goals and 7 assists across 24 games, the total amount per team in the league's inaugural season. She had a plus/minus rating of +11, which means she contributed to eleven more goals on the ice than were scored against her team while she was playing. At 33 years old, Natalie Spooner is one of the oldest players in the PWHL, however she contributed heavily to the team, who finished the regular season ranked first, but did not win the playoffs.
This chart shows Spooner's stats from last season. She should perform relatively similar this year, however she will be out for the start of the season as she is recovering from ACL surgery. Due to this change, I would predict she will not be in the top 5 players in this 2024-25 season.
